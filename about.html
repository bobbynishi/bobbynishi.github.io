Sr. Software Engineer

My passion is storytelling. I tell stories through user experiences.

Sometimes it’s through graphic design, motion graphics or sometimes it’s through front-end web applications.

Design should be simple. Intuitive. Comprehensive.

My formal education is in Cinematography and lighting for the moving image. But I always was fascinated by interactive design.
